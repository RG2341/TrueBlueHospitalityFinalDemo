About Us

Our mission is to provide exceptional hospitality services with integrity and innovation, focusing on achieving excellence for our owners, investors, and clients. Through hands-on management, we aim to delight guests, empower employees, and maximize investment value.

Meet Our True Blue Team:

David Kalik

CEO & Founder

Mr. Kalik has more than forty years experience in the hospitality industry in various capacities. Mr. Kalik focuses his skills on revenue generation, expense controls, guest satisfaction, human resources techniques, capital planning and long term vision plans for all properties he manages. He is an actively involved operator, constantly communicating to and directing the managers of his properties to strive to be the best hoteliers and restaurateurs they can be.

Ben Creasy

Director of Operations

Experienced hotelier with success in the hospitality industry, Mr. Creasy currently manages four hotels and seven commercial/residential properties. He presents keen skills in P&L management, sales, customer/tenant satisfaction, and operational technologies. He takes pride in running exemplary day to day operations, and consistently exceeds the expectations of guests and owners alike. He fosters a superior culture where team members want to work. Mr. Creasy drives growth for all properties under his management.

DJ Gajjar

Director of Capital Projects

Mr. Gajjar has over twenty-four years of experience in ownership, management, and hotel operations. He excels in overseeing brand standard implementation, renovations and its budgeting, and the integration of advanced technology in hotels. In addition to his technical and operational expertise, Mr. Gajjar places an emphasis on positive employee relations, understanding that a motivated and supported team is crucial to delivering exceptional guest experiences.

Amy Houchins

Director of Sales

Born and bred in Charleston, SC, Ms. Houchins brings a lifetime of hospitality expertise to every endeavor. Her early days were spent exploring the cobblestone streets and taking in the city's rich history. Ms. Houchins has a proven track record of increasing revenue through innovative marketing strategies and operational excellence. With a commitment to maximizing profitability, Ms. Houchins looks forward to leveraging her expertise to boost the financial success of every property she manages.
